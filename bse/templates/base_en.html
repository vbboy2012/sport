Credit mechanisms

  • Degree of Trust

    Degree of Trust is the ratio between sum of all locking tokens periods and all existing tokens.

  • Voting

    Technically, a voting mechanism is written in a Smart Contract, that contains transparent rules and conditions to execute an action or to grant some permission or privilege.

  • Voting quorum and timeframe

    n order for voting to be valid, a minimum quorum of addresses, that cumulatively own at least 20% of all tokens in existence, must submit their vote.All votings must last for at least a 14 days period.

  • Right to vote, Right to be elected

    Each address have the right to vote proportionally to the amount of donated or locked coins (PoT) from that address.Each address have the right to be elected, respectively to gain Trust Level and underlying rights.

  • Elections

    Elections will be triggered each half-year.An election list of all candidates will be collected and locked one month (31 days) before the election day.

  • Trust Levels

    DAO has 3 Trust Levels：Executive Board(EB)、Elected Commons(EC)、Voters。

  • Identity disclosure

    Executive Board (EB) candidates must disclose their Identity to ensure that all 5 addresses belong to different persons.

  • Management of Funds

    The tokens of the community fund and the technical fund are permanently locked.Only from the DAOClub intelligent contract can be used for the purpose of the fund.

OUR VISION

Today, sports events have bene far beyond its inherent scope. Currently, commercial development depending on sports events is growing vigorously. Economic benefits from sports rebroadcast and court advertisements have been very huge. As can be said, sports events have become a key carrier of commercial operation and economic benefit creation in the market economic system.

A democratic and autonomous organization and economic ecological circle will be established specific to sports events by DAOClub, and sports events will be shifted to the economic model of digital asset,Serve the world's sports enthusiasts.

In addition to the centralization prediction network, DAOClub DAO also includes the issuance of value added funds and value shares. All these are driven by democracy, not decided by one person or one team, but by the whole DAOClub community. DAOClub will be a democratic and win-win community.
